Step 1 — Before you begin
Sit with Arjun in a quiet place. Reduce distractions — switch off the TV. The session is only 4–5 minutes.

Press "Begin activity" below. The app will show Arjun 4 coloured objects. A Malayalam voice will ask him to tap the correct one. Your job is to sit beside him and gently encourage — don't answer for him.

When the activity ends, you'll see a quick 5-question form about how it went. This takes 30 seconds and helps the teacher plan tomorrow's lesson.
